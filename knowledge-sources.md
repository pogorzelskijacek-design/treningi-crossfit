# Knowledge Sources — the coach's curated "curriculum"

This is the vetted, **legal** starter set of sources the AI coach will be grounded in
(Retrieval-Augmented Generation / RAG). The goal is a coach whose advice is **based on
concrete, citable sources** — not just a language model's own pretrained knowledge.

These same entries seed the in-app **Knowledge** tab (`src/data/knowledgeSources.ts`).
Add your own sources there to keep "teaching" the coach.

## Legal principle

- ✅ Use **official / freely-available** versions (the links below), and material **you
  legally own** and upload for personal use.
- ❌ Do **not** use pirated copies of paid books (Scribd, pdfcoffee, etc.).
- 📚 Paid books worth buying later, then uploading: *Overcoming Gravity* (Steven Low),
  *Scientific Principles of Hypertrophy Training* (RP), *Olympic Weightlifting* (Greg Everett).
- ⚠️ Respect each site's terms when ingesting content automatically.

---

## A. CrossFit methodology
- **CrossFit Level 1 Training Guide** — foundational methodology (movement standards,
  programming, "What is Fitness?"). The single most important source.
  <https://library.crossfit.com/free/pdf/CFJ_English_Level1_TrainingGuide.pdf> · free (official)
- **CrossFit.com** — daily WOD archive + free technique articles/videos.
  <https://www.crossfit.com/> · free

## B. Olympic lifting / technique
- **Catalyst Athletics** (Greg Everett) — largest free weightlifting library: 600+ exercise
  demos, 1300+ articles, programs. <https://www.catalystathletics.com/> · free

## C. Strength & hypertrophy (evidence-based)
- **Stronger by Science** (Greg Nuckols) — deeply-researched articles + free Complete
  Strength Training Guide. <https://www.strongerbyscience.com/complete-strength-training-guide/> · free
- **Barbell Medicine** (Dr. J. Feigenbaum, A. Baraki) — strength, pain, rehab; blog + podcast.
  <https://www.barbellmedicine.com/blog/> · free
- **Renaissance Periodization** (Dr. Mike Israetel) — hypertrophy & volume landmarks
  (MEV/MAV/MRV). <https://rpstrength.com/blogs/articles> · articles free (books paid)

## D. Gymnastics / calisthenics
- **r/bodyweightfitness Wiki & Recommended Routine** — community-vetted progressions.
  <https://www.reddit.com/r/bodyweightfitness/wiki/index/> · free

## E. Endurance / Hyrox
- **Uphill Athlete** (Steve House / Scott Johnston) — aerobic base & endurance periodization.
  <https://uphillathlete.com/blogs/all-articles> · free
- **HYROX (official)** — station standards, rules, training guidance. <https://hyrox.com/> · free

## F. Nutrition (evidence-based)
- **Examine.com** — independent evidence database on nutrition & supplements.
  <https://examine.com/> · partly free
- **ISSN Position Stands (JISSN)** — peer-reviewed, open-access: protein, creatine, timing, caffeine.
  <https://jissn.biomedcentral.com/> · open access
- **NIH Office of Dietary Supplements** — authoritative government fact sheets.
  <https://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/list-all/> · free
- **Precision Nutrition — blog** — large library of practical, evidence-based coaching articles.
  <https://www.precisionnutrition.com/blog> · free
- **MacroFactor — Knowledge Base** (SBS team) — macros, energy balance, workout nutrition.
  <https://macrofactor.com/articles/> · free

## G. Mobility / rehab / injury prevention
- **E3 Rehab** — evidence-based rehab & injury-risk reduction (Doctors of Physical Therapy).
  <https://e3rehab.com/> · free
- **Squat University** (Dr. Aaron Horschig) — mobility & lifting mechanics, working around pain.
  <https://squatuniversity.com/> · free
- **The Prehab Guys** — prehab/rehab & injury prevention from physios. <https://theprehabguys.com/> · mostly free

## H. Sleep & recovery
- **Sleep Foundation** — authoritative sleep & sleep-hygiene reference. <https://www.sleepfoundation.org/> · free
- **Huberman Lab — Toolkit for Sleep** — actionable sleep-optimization protocol + recovery podcast.
  <https://www.hubermanlab.com/newsletter/toolkit-for-sleep> · free

## I. Sport psychology
- **AASP — Association for Applied Sport Psychology** — free resources & blog for athletes/coaches.
  <https://appliedsportpsych.org/resources/> · free
- **APA Division 47** — Society for Sport, Exercise & Performance Psychology. <https://www.apadivisions.org/division-47> · free

## J. Podcasts (public — transcribe episodes)
- **Iron Culture** (Eric Helms & Omar Isuf) — evidence-based lifting & nutrition. Find in your podcast app.
- **Barbell Medicine Podcast** — <https://www.barbellmedicine.com/podcast/> · free
